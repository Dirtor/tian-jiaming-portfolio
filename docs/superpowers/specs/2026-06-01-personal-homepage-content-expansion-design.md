# Personal Homepage Content Expansion Design

Date: 2026-06-01

## Goal

Strengthen Tian Jiaming's personal homepage without changing the approved first impression.

The current hero, visual tone, avatar, and black / white / warm gray / orange-red palette should remain. The homepage should continue to feel like a personal website, not a recruiting page and not a pure fundraising deck.

The content upgrade should solve one problem:

> The site has a strong identity, but the evidence layer after the hero is not yet dense enough.

Primary audience:
- Investors and financing-related contacts.
- Business collaboration partners.

Secondary future audience:
- Recruiters or hiring managers, but job-search signals should not appear for now.

## Non-Goals

- Do not reposition the page as "open to work".
- Do not add target job titles.
- Do not make the homepage look like a conventional resume template.
- Do not publish private fundraising details, exact investor names, unapproved screenshots, unfinished UI, phone number, or non-public customer details.
- Do not change the hero direction or color system.
